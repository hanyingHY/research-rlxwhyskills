# Lite Workspace Progress Audit

## Objective

Tell the difference between a scaffolded lite packet and a genuinely useful decision packet.

## Suggested status levels

1. `structural_only`
2. `evidence_building`
3. `decision_ready`

## structural_only

Use this when the files exist but the source index, retained evidence, action map, and summary are still mostly empty placeholders.

## evidence_building

Use this when the lite packet has real sources or retained evidence but the action mapping and summary are still weak.

## decision_ready

Use this when the summary memo, action map, and next-user-decision surface are strong enough for a real operator choice.

## Escalation signal

If the lite packet is becoming large, contradictory, or tightly tied to expensive validation, treat that as a signal to escalate into the full research pipeline.
