# Solution Modes

## Objective

Let the user choose between a stable shortlist and an aggressive shortlist after the lite pass is complete.

## Stable mode

Prefer:

1. direct evidence
2. simpler ideas
3. lower execution risk

## Aggressive mode

Prefer:

1. higher-upside ideas
2. more novel or transfer-heavy ideas
3. options that may need stronger follow-up validation

## Rule

Even in lite mode, do not decide this silently for the user.

Show both views and let the user choose.
