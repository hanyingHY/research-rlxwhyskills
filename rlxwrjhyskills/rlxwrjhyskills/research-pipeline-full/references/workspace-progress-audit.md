# Workspace Progress Audit

## Objective

Distinguish a merely initialized workspace from one that is actually producing decision-grade research.

## Suggested status levels

1. `structural_only`
2. `evidence_building`
3. `decision_ready`
4. `launch_candidate`

## structural_only

Use this when the scaffold exists but the evidence rows, reports, and route boards are still placeholders or nearly empty.

## evidence_building

Use this when the workspace has real source intake or claim extraction, but route ranking, synthesis, and next-step control are still weak.

## decision_ready

Use this when retained evidence, synthesis artifacts, and route boards are strong enough for a real user decision.

## launch_candidate

Use this when direction evidence, shared tables, route boards, and prompt-pack surfaces are strong enough to support coordinated worker launch or execution planning.

## Rule

Do not call a workspace ready just because the files exist.

The audit should look for real content density, not only structural presence.
